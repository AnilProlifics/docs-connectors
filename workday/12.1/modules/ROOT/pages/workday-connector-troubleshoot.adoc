= Troubleshoot Workday Connector

== Timeout in Studio

Sometimes, you might experience a timeout in Anypoint Studio while obtaining metadata from Workday Connector.  

=== Cause 

This can happen due to connectivity issues. 

=== Symptoms

=== Resolution

When one of these errors occurs, follow these steps to increase the timeout in Anypoint Studio:

. In Anypoint Studio, navigate to *Preferences > Anypoint Studio > Tooling*.
. Increase the values to double the default value for `Default Connection Timeout` and `Default Read Timeout`.
. Click *Apply*, and restart Studio.
