. Add the connector to your Studio project

== Add the Connector to Your Project
Anypoint Studio provides two ways to add the connector to your Studio project: from
the Exchange button in the Studio taskbar or from the Mule Palette view.
=== Add the Connector Using Exchange
. In Studio, create a Mule project.
. Click the Exchange icon *(X)* in the upper-left of the Studio task bar.
. In Exchange, click *Login* and supply your Anypoint Platform username and password.
. In Exchange, search for AS2 Connector.
. Select the AS2 Connector connector and click *Add to project*.
. Follow the prompts to install the connector.
=== Add the Connector in Studio
. In Studio, create a Mule project.
. In the Mule Palette view, click *(X) Search in Exchange*.
. In *Add Modules to Project*, type AS2 Connector in the search field.
. Click the AS2 Connector connector name in *Available modules*.
. Click *Add*.
. Click *Finish*.
